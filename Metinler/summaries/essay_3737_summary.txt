the fellowship of christian athletes fca is among the largest sports ministries in the world.
the fca is a nonprofit christian organization that leverages popular sports to spread christianity.
internationally, the fca strategy offers numerous opportunities for members through vision, training, and sports trips.
the fca sends members to other countries to offer sports ministry training to various groups through the training trips it organizes.
all these camps and several others help develop and nurture the talents of various members of the fca, be it the coaches or athletes.
