various prospective payment systems resemble each other because they are all based on reimbursement, which helps cover the expenses associated with caring for the patients.
the most important principle associated with these systems is that the payment is preagreed and is identified on the basis of the evaluation of the particular procedures and operations harrington, 2021.
the home health prospective payment system, on the other hand, is based on the principle that all the procedures should follow the requirements associated with home care.
