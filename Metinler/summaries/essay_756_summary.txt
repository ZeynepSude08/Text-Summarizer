the allegory of the cave by plato is a work about how people perceive life and reality.
first of all, plato created the people in the cave captives in order to rhetorically appeal to the audiences emotions and arouse the sensations that already exist in them, which, of course, already produces an intimidating image in the readers mind.
to express his argument, plato also used the rhetorical method of rhetorical questions.
dont you think he would be puzzled plato these are among other examples where plato employs the rhetorical questions.
