charles boldens influence on jazz music essay
charles bolden was born in new orleans and is recognized as the first person to perform and play what could thereafter be considered jazz music.
many aspects of bolden traditions, such as syncopation, polyrhythm, and improvision, linger today and are considered significant to jazz music nmaam, 2019.
bolden later joined a new orleans band led by charles galloway, where he displayed his musical talent and skills.
brass bands that performed various secular and religious functions also helped shape boldens life in music.
