critical analysis of dagoberto gilbs love in l.a. essay
the purpose of this essay is a literary analysis of dagoberto gilbs love in l.a. using critical methods.
in the case of love in l.a., the reader has the opportunity to observe just one morning in the life of the protagonist, a man named jake gilb.
first of all, readers can observe the protagonists thoughts, but only his, which forces them to form an opinion about the character according to his actions in the current context.
continuing to look at love in l.a. from the point of view of a formalist critic, one should also pay attention to the figure of the protagonist.
