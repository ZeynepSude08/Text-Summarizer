science and the environment plastics and microplastics report
in 1907, the first fully synthetic plastic was invented by leo baekeland and revolutionized mass manufacturing science history institute, n.d..
during the second world war, plastic production increased by 300 as it was used to make military equipment such as helmets, raincoats, tents, and parachutes science history institute, n.d..
