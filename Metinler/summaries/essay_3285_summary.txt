this paper hypothesizes that along with the overload of health systems, covid times will be remembered as a period when new lifestyles were elaborated with stress on increased digitalization in all spheres of life.
thus, while earlier, people used to gather together to have a good time, today, due to covids limitations, many of us go to online chat rooms to exchange opinions and state our views.
thus, the covid pandemic brought about digitalization in all spheres of life, which will remain long after the pandemic today, new digital trends determine the vector of development.
