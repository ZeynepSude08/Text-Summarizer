this essay aims to discuss the connection between phones and teenagers mental health and explain why the use of smartphones is merely a reflection of problems that children would face either way.
some people believe that screen time causes anxiety, depression, stress, and mental problems in teenagers.
in this regard, the use of phones as a means of communication and a source of knowledge and valuable information can support children in their worries.
to summarize, there are two opposing opinions with regard to the effect of phone use on children and teenagers.
