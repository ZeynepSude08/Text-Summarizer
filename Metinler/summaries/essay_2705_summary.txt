the bibles impact on modern education and society essay
vishal mangalwadi opines how the bible shaped and molded the western civilizations throughout the ages up to the modern state that the world is in his book the book that made your world.
mangalwadi 2012 states that neither the imperialists nor the merchants advanced education, but the advancement of knowledge was carried out through the efforts of christian missionaries.
modern churches continue to spread education where it has not reached through the development of schools and universities the world over.
modern education systems are built upon the early teachings and ways that were developed by early missionaries.
