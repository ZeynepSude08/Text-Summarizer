there are several types of ids, the main ones being hids and nids.
hids protects files even if the administrator is disconnected from the lan because hids is installed on the device directly.
in the case of using hids and nids, the first one will provide protection in a complex, and the second one will solve local issues magalhaes, 2018.
full data protection requires comprehensive analysis that hids and nids can provide, but this requires special care on the part of administrators.
