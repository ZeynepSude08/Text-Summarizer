colon cancer symptoms, genes, and immunosuppression case study
mutations in the pole and pold1 gene pairs cause familial colon cancer.
the macc1 gene stimulates the expression of hepatocyte growth factor, which is linked to colon cancer metastasis bateson, 2017.
immunosuppression impairs the immune systems ability to recognize and fight cancer cells, as well as infections caused by cancer lopez et al., 2017.
