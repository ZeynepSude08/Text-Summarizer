2. concept 1 the gloried self
over the last few years, u.s. police officers have been involved in multiple cases of violence against civilians, including the deadly use of firearms.
concept 1 the gloried self
back in time, u.s. police officers were portrayed in a largely positive light as vigilant defenders of law and order.
however, the concept of gloried self is likely to work in the opposite way as well.
working in law enforcement is stressful in this regard, police officers can be compared to healthcare professionals, such as surgeons.
police officers may shoot a civilian out of better safe than sorry thinking in order to ensure their survival in unclear circumstances.
