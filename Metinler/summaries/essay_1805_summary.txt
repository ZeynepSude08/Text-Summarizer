by and whole, the lockdown had a severe impact on the people of the uk, leaving the public struggling to earn funds.
to begin with, as people were fired from their jobs, they had to seek other opportunities to earn money, which was highly challenging due to the unfavorable economic situation for businesses.
according to office for national statistics 2021, 1.771 million people have lost their jobs since 2019, the beginning of the lockdown.
