halloween , the 1978 movie directed by john carpenter, is considered to be an alltime classic of the horror genre.
if a films greatness is defined by the combination of popularity and significance, then halloween is arguably, the most successful horror film in american history knppler, 2017, p.212.
the reason why it is such a remarkable movie is the way halloween satisfies the spectator who came to see the horror story by building up the tension and then releasing it via various tools and decisions.
halloween as a classic example of the killer genre
since the plot of the movies of the killer genre revolves around the murderer, it is only fair that the way his presence and actions are depicted is what makes the story horrifying.
all that further establishes the killer as something different, which is often present in the monster genre of horror.
