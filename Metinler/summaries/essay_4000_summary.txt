bach and stravinsky the pioneers of musical composition essay
on the one hand, bachs joy of mans desiring is an intellectually rigorous composition that is transcendent in terms of how it unfolds the beauty of music through bachs signature style.
even though the ballet contradicted stravinskys music just as bachs prelude interfered with the choral section, their innovative approaches still generated a perfect response from the audience.
from my point of view, this multilayered structure of the rite of spring became the signature element of stravinskys musical compositions because it was both classical and exotic at the same time.
from music to dancing, it allowed stravinsky to create unique rhythmic components that unified simpler fragments into complex compositions.
in rite of spring , i witnessed how the internal disorder created by stravinsky turned into a larger melodic composition on the exterior.
