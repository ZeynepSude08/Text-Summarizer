michelangelos sistine chapel ceiling depicting stories from the book of genesis is considered one of the greatest examples of renaissance painting.
the painting reflects religious motives and reminds one of christs plans that sinners need help, not the enlightened ones.
elevation of the cross by rubens is a biblical depiction of the crucifixion of christ following the specific guidelines in the field of art developed by the trident cathedral.
the elevation of the altar cross was the first order that rubens received after returning to antwerp from his italian stay, which is why there were many religious and italian influences in work wilke, 2017.
from all of the above, the historical events towards reformation in churches radically influenced the increase in religious motives in art.
