the literal meaning of the scriptures is revealed by rigorous examination of the text utilizing all available interpretative tools, such as archaeological data, historical, and literary analysis martin, 2006.
true comprehension of the substance of texts, rather than a mere acceptance of literal meaning, is required for an allegorical reading of scripture sri, 1996.
the allegorical sense is useful in avoiding the trivial, nonsensical, and redundant meanings that are pretty much inevitable when reading scripture literally.
