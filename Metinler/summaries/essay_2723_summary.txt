the components of the public health infrastructure essay
the quality of the delivered healthcare services depends on the quality of public health infrastructure that consists of hospitals and laboratories, medical staff members, equipment, and even information systems.
thus, even though installation of such components of public health infrastructure as advanced equipment is costly, it helps to increase the quality of the delivered care and, therefore, improves public health conditions all over the nation.
to conclude, out of the three components of public health infrastructure, the most important one is human resources.
