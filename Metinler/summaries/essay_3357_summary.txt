its basically just immoral to be rich by smith essay
the essay in question speculates that being rich as a tremendous privilege that is expected to be sacrificed for the benefit of those less fortunate conflicts with the intrinsic need for maintaining ones social status and access to multiple resources.
in turn, expecting that people of significant affluence should give up their wealth to support the impoverished would imply promoting additional injustice since a large number of rich people have gained their status due to their efforts and persistence.
