even though durer had never seen a live rhino and depicted it inaccurately, durers rhinoceros became one of the most popular pictures of the time.
the fact that durer was interested in the animal and many people bought woodcut pictures of the rhino show how interested the public was in the gift from the east.
the story of the rhinoceros travels demonstrates the diplomatic interests of india, the islamic world, and europe, as the animal was first gifted by indians to an islamic king, then to a european king, and then to the pope.
