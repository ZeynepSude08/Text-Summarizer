2. cultural appropriation in the digital society
media stories promote cultural appropriation by sharing values, images, myths, and symbols of a given culture with society kellner 12.
cultural appropriation is unavoidable in society as people try to copy values, images, and ideas of other cultures.
people from one culture may copy the other culture on their clothing hence leading to appropriation in fashion and design.
content appropriation is common in society because artists will have to use some content from a given culture to present their musical and poetry content.
people have a different perspective on culture hence appropriation may lead to misunderstanding.
cultural appropriation can be good when the participants copy the right content and ideas.
one of the negative impacts of cultural appropriation is copying immoral content from the other culture.
