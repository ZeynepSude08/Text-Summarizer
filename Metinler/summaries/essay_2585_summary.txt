revenue cycle management rcm is a financial process used by healthcare institutions to facilitate tracking patient care events from when they register to the time they make payment for the rendered services.
rcm reminds payers insurance companies and patients of existing balances, informs clients about upcoming appointments with doctors, and questions the insurers when claims are denied healthcare financial management association, 2018.
rcm allows healthcare providers the enter information, such as patients names and type of treatment received, needed for claim processing without resubmission or revision of the latter.
ineffective rcm lessens the amount of revenue a healthcare institution generates due to possible unprocessed payments and denied claims healthcare financial management association, 2018.
conclusively, rcm is an instrumental tool that facilitates tracking patient care events from the time they register they make payment for the rendered services.
