the impact of atmospheric pollution on human health and the environment essay
atmospheric pollution is a set of environmental problems associated with releasing chemicals and accumulating concentrations of natural gases in the air.
industrial enterprises, transport, power plants, and heating systems emit tons of dangerous pollutants thus, environmental damage is extensive and multifaceted, affecting all living things on the planet.
in order to monitor the state of the environment, a comprehensive indicator has been introduced that characterizes the degree of atmospheric pollution brusseau 298.
