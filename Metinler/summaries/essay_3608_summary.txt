the 17article constitution is based on the people of japan however, some nonjapanese influences have been mentioned in the law code.
for instance, in article 3 of the shotoku taishi 604 constitution, the imperials who are nonjapanese influence the people of japan.
in that, just as the lord, who is heaven commands its subject, should obey, the people of japan should pay heed to their imperial powers and submit to avoid harsh consequences, which are otherwise termed as a ruin in the article.
