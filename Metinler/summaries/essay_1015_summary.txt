alternative medicine as a field for developing cultural competency essay
the cultural influence of alternative medicine can be very strong and nurses must take that into account.
female patients may be directed to take alternative medicine by their male relatives and nurses should understand such arrangements to offer targeted guidance.
ones ethnic and cultural background might influence ones views, and patients may use complementary medicine under the influence of society.
this move will ensure that patients using alternative medicine are looked after and cared for by professionals who know exactly what they have to handle.
moreover, the training that nurses are to undergo must be restructured to be culturally congruent to expose care providers to different cultural contexts making them better prepared to communicate with people from diverse social backgrounds foronda et al., 2018.
