sex and love in ackermans a fine, a private place and olds sex without love essay
this essay examines two poems by ackermans a fine, a private place and olds sex without love.
the central thesis of this work is that olds shows sex without love as a beautiful wrapper, devoid of meaning, truth, and any warm emotions, while ackerman, on the contrary, demonstrates the positive feelings caused by sex without love in the context of a long relationship.
