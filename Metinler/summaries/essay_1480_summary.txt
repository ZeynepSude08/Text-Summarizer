medical malpractice and ethical guidelines case study
this essay argues on the example of deathresulted medical case that deontological ethics and shared decisionmaking can highly mitigate the number of malpractice instances.
the iturralde vs hilo medical center case provides a vivid example of violating core medical ethics principles.
the reason is that modern research shows that the involvement of patients in decisionmaking is associated with better outcomes alden et al., 2013.
the deontological ethics and open access to all documents will ensure the accountability of physicians to the public.
the main conclusions of the essay are that the deontological approach to ethics and physicians openminded attitude toward patients are crucial for the effective work of medical facilities.
