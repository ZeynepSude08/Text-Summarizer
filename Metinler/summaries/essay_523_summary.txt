midlife crisis in tolstoys my confession essay
this paper reviews leo tolstoys my confession as an illustration of the inner struggle experienced by a man in his midlife crisis.
ultimately, the author faced an imminent crisis of meaning at the figurative middle point in life.
nevertheless, as tolstoy passed the middle point of his life, materialistic wealth had not ensured his full satisfaction.
thus, tolstoy encountered a midlife crisis of meaning, knowing what he accomplished but questioning whether it was worth it.
tolstoy 2018 inferred that this knowledge provided him with facts but failed to ensure the meaning of life.
in this regard, a midlife crisis can, indeed, be addressed through tolstoys solution, although it requires further development to be universal.
