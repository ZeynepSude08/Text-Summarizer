night shift nurses breaks in hospitals research paper
2021 discusses nurses experiences taking breaks on the night shift.
2021 was to describe and understand the lived experience of night shift nurses taking breaks in hospitals, as well as the implications of this phenomenon in the workplace.
the datas primary themes show that night shift nurses do not take advantage of their breaks to the fullest extent possible in order to restore or improve their health landis et al., 2021.
because of irregular breaks during night shifts, nurses not only threaten their productivity and efficiency during the work with patients but at the same time put their health at risk.
