in his essay the kind of world we all want maybe, just maybe, we can stop the greatest propaganda machine in history, sasha baron cohen speaks of his fears about the impact that social media may have on the promotion of conspiracy theories and prejudices.
although cohens fears could be seen as farfetched, the overall tone of the essay allows keeping the focus on an important social issue, namely, that one of the misuse of social media as the tool for fueling the social divide.
despite the fact that cohens essay contains several ideas that could be interpreted as overly exaggerated, its tone and the use of pathos, ethos, and logos along with literary devices such as irony and symbolism, serve to convey the main idea exceptionally well.
