the idea of the classical school is that people have free will when making decisions, and punishment is a deterrent for them when committing a crime.
one such idea is the biological theory of crime which was created by the italian forensic psychiatrist cesare lombroso dunnage 1.
psychological theories of crime basically contain the idea that the urge to commit a crime is dictated by the psychology of people and that all people are deviants by nature.
according to durkheims theory of social disorganization, crime is a normal phenomenon in society, which is unpleasant, but without which the existence of humanity is impossible bellair 2.
strain theory considers that the tension that was caused by certain factors in society, such as low wages or the unavailability of a good education, is a factor that encourages people to commit crimes.
