uber appbased technologies in supply chain essay
webbased applications such as uber freight, magaya supply chain, keeptruckin, shipsy, amazon, and cargomatic, among others, have reduced the cycle time within supply chain management in an unprecedented manner.
this paper will look at the pros and cons of webbased applications such as uber freight, how the apps will continue to reduce the cycle time within the supply chain and how they will continue to affect world businesses.
like all things that deal with technology, appbased technologies will continue to provide massive advantages but at a cost.
the webbased applications will also, in addition to reducing costs, offer better customer service.
the technology will also be of use for the employees in logging bills through the applications.
