modern internet and hate speech essay
the problem of hate speech on the internet is acute and will not fade away in the coming years.
one of the significant reasons hate speech on the internet and in general in the media is quickly and firmly entrenched in its latent resolution for most people.
hate speech makes the internet less sterile and more diverse by showing other people how different human views and opinions can be.
the topic of hate speech was generated by the democratization of the internet and has become its central paradox, requiring the discussion of different views.
arguments for hate speech as an acceptable option for communication on the internet refer to freedom from emotions, diversity, and, obviously, the fundamental freedom of speech.
the topic of hate speech affects the cultural, social, political, and psychological aspects therefore, along with the complex problem of bullying on the internet, it requires a very detailed study in the future.
