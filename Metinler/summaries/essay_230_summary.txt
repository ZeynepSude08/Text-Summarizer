nursing practices that reduce patient falls
nurses have vital roles to play in managing, and reducing patient falls within the facilities.
having a deep understanding of the facilities, nurses can offer education and proper information to the children and related parties about the possible cause of patients falls.
moreover, nurses can assess the effects of medication to influence the possibility of a patient falling.
encouraging the implementation of the assessment tool would make nurses insight into possible areas that can increase pediatric falls in the facility.
