lisa black thomas examines the situation in the article stress and depression in undergraduate students during the covid19 pandemic nursing students compared to undergraduate students in nonnursing majors.
the study aims to determine how different the manifestations of stress and depression are among american nursing students compared to students of other disciplines and what supports nursing students in continuing their education.
the quantitative data analysis showed that nursing students felt stress and changes in educational processes due to the pandemic more than students in other disciplines.
