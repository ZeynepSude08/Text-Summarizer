the research conducted by rosen and nofziger 2019 aimed to investigate how bullying reinforced hegemonic masculinity.
rosen and nofziger 2019 applied a quantitative research design to explore the relationships between students bullying experiences and race, age, and socioeconomic status and identify the frequency of bullying.
the authors used a qualitative method of content analysis to find common themes in students bullying experiences, which would be related to hegemonic masculinity.
