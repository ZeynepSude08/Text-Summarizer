transgender women should be allowed to compete in olympic sports essay
the discussion of whether transgender women should be allowed into the competitive sport, specifically olympic, has reached its peak in recent times.
therefore the essay aims to analyze the situation based on credible and scholarly sources, defining arguments for and against the participation of transgender women in olympic sports.
male transgender athletes, for instance, are freely allowed to participate in both mens and womens sports without any additional procedures.
therefore, until proven otherwise, transgender females have to be allowed to participate in olympic sport as any other woman without prejudices and discrimination.
