1. the caribbean and middle east nationalities
although the caribbean, arab, and other middle east nationalities can be considered coming from almost the same region, their cultural and economic differences are quite obvious.
on this matter, arabic, middle east, and caribbean nationalities show similar behavioral patterns when it comes to their adjustment abroad.
compared with arabic nationalities who are known for their prejudice and reserved, reticent attitude towards foreigners, the inhabitants of the caribbean region have higher chances of being successful abroad.
