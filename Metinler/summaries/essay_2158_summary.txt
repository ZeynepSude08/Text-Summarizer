databases dbs are used to store large amounts of data of a variety of types.
these databases can duplicate the same data in multiple locations, allowing it to be accessed quickly from anywhere and serve as backups in case one location has a malfunction.
while some attacks against a db are performed without legitimate access, one can also abuse legitimate privileges to corrupt or steal data.
without legitimate access rights, an attacker can still gain information from a database.
for instance, an attacker can gain sensitive information if he or she can access accelerometer or eye tracking data.
inference controls are methods of protecting sensitive data from this attack malik  patel, 2016.
an injection is a type of attack where a user manipulates legitimate input to execute a db query.
