the waiting room is a documentary film based on the daily experiences of doctors working in american public hospitals.
the film reflects on the daily life at american public hospitals, especially for uninsured patients.
through the film, the audience can learn about the challenges patients face, visiting american public hospitals, who are in dire need of medical attention.
the patients featured in the film present the excruciating pains and frustrations the patients without insurance covers face in american public hospitals.
the film, set at highland hospital in oakland, reflects on the daily patients experiences in public hospitals.
