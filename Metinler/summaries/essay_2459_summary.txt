nonmaleficence as ethical principle in healthcare essay
the fundamental principles of ethics that laid down the activities basis for medical personnel are nonmaleficence, beneficence, justice, and autonomy varkey, 2021.
the principle of nonmaleficence is reflected in the hippocratic oath in words, first do no harm summers, 2009, p.
despite all the aspirations of specialists to comply with ethical principles, their workload and sometimes character can lead to a violation of nonmaleficence.
