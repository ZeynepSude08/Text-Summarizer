2. advantages of act utilitarianism and virtue ethics
act utilitarianism and virtue ethics are moral theories that justify and help reflect on the ethical decisions made.
act utilitarianism is better than virtue ethics since it has the majority in mind.
therefore, act utilitarianism and virtue ethics can be used to complement each other.
act utilitarianism is better than virtue ethics since it is democratic and impartial.
combining act utilitarianism and virtue ethics gives an adequate theory to lead to informed and morally good decisions.
