sex trafficking, public health and sustainability essay
addressing the issue of sex trafficking now is extremely important all over the world, as trafficking in persons has become one of the most topical global concerns and serious threats to public health.
public health and sustainability are closely related to sex trafficking, and effective policies to eliminate this threat as well as help the victims of exploitation are essential to ensure the successful accomplishment of sustainable development goals.
other horrific problems caused by sex trafficking include unsafe abortion and infections resulting from unsanitary conditions the victims had to live in or medical procedures conducted while they were forced to engage in sexual activities.
