gary sotos afterlife and magical realism essay
chuy, or jesus, the main character in gary sotos the afterlife , is a fifteenyearold boy whose sudden and senseless death shows others the value of cherishing the present.
such a technique of combining reality and the afterlife makes the readers lose themselves between the two worlds, empathizing with the main characters as if their fate could be redeemed.
the primary purpose of gary sotos the afterlife is to show the significance of human life and forgiveness from the perspective of those who lose it.
