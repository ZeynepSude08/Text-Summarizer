A Charles Schwab Corporation Case Essay aims to analyze the form of business in the CSC. The CSC is a corporation with several subsidiaries and conducts business following the requirements for a corporation form of organization. In the corporation, shareholders are free to transfer ownership, which means that in critical situations such as bankruptcy, the corporation can be sold to another owner.
