----- ÖZET -----
Code of Practice is applied to major hazard installations identified on the basis of a list of hazardous substances associated with threshold quantities. According to the code, by arranging separation between large centers of population, including housing, shops, schools, and hospitals, major accidents that may occur at these installations will be prevented.
