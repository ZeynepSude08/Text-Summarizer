----- ÖZET -----
Beowulf may be regarded as one of the most prominent ancient European poems. The poem describes the glorious life and deeds of a hero, Beowulf, who was regarded as a person sent by heaven for people. The main hero desires personal glory and has nothing to lose. After becoming a king, he realizes the importance of being a wise ruler.
