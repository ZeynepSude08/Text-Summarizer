Casa Mono is a tapas bar restaurant inspired by the cuisine of Spain’s Costa Brava. Founded in 2003 by Mario Batali and Joe Bastianich, Casa Mono was one of the earliest and most influential restaurants to transform the Spanish tapas paradigm. This essay will examine numerous ways this restaurant brings novelty to the world of Spanish cuisine while staying true to its authenticity.
