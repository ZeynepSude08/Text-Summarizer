----- ÖZET -----
Emergency Management is an organizational task entrusted with developing a structure within the population to decrease exposure to risks. Forest fires, tremors, hurricanes, accidents, bombings, dangerous chemicals events, snowstorms, and flooding are just a few of the typical calamities that come under emergency management.
