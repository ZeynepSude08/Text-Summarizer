Chronic pain is still considered to be an extreme issue among at least 8% of the residents of progressive countries. This research paper aims to analyze levels of depression and anxiety among people receiving therapy by comparing the effects of pharmaceutical opioids and medical marijuana. Participants were recruited for six months at two major pain relief centers in Israel.
