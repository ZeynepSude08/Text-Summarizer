----- ÖZET -----
Aging refers to the progressive deterioration in physiologic functions within the human body that occur over time. This essay is an analysis of an interview conducted on an aged woman to assess the changes attributed to aging. The first part analyzes the interview and the responses provided by the woman including the variations in her body functions. The second part is a summary of the information available on the changes that occur in the integumentary system with age.
