Consequentialism is an ethical philosophy that determines whether something is right or wrong based on its repercussions. The consequentialist argument that focuses on euthanasia and physician-assisted suicide stresses the importance of the repercussions of the given practice. Weinstock stresses consequentialist concerns to regulate and control the activity.
