----- ÖZET -----
Customer-Oriented Service in Maritime Freight Transportation Research Paper. The paper explores an idea of customer-oriented transportation services. The primary emphasis is laid on the client’s needs being met rather than the immediate economic gains from a trade agreement. The core benefit of this model is associated with a higher lifetime value of a customer.
