Environmental sustainability refers to the point where renewable resource harvesting rate should not surpass that of regeneration. This entails the lowering of economic impacts that might be unfavorable to the environment, as well as designing a business that reduces the use of carbon. One of the approaches in achieving environmental sustainability is by viewing people as a part of the ecosystem.
