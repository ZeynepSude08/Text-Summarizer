The research involved finding the difference between men and women about body image concerns. The research included 700 participants from Princeton University who completed survey questionnaires. 64% of the participants were women, and 36% were men allowed to complete various measures such as the DMS, BSQ, and BSRI for a personality trait. The results showed that men recorded higher on DMS and women indicated higher scores on BSQS.
