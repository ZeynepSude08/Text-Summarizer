Carbohydrates are energy-giving foods that enable the body to function well. Scientists recommend that carbohydrates form between 45% and 65% of one’s daily consumption. High carbohydrates uptake translates to high calories and sufficient energy for the body. Consumption of high amounts of starch and fat causes obesity.
