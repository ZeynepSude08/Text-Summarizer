Pediatric falls are the unintentional fall of hospitalized children, resulting in injury or related emergencies. The relative estimated pediatric falls are about 0.58 to 2.2 per 1000 patients. The study will examine the effects of patient falls and techniques that hospitals and nurses can employ to curve the challenge.
