Guilty but mentally ill (GBMI) verdict authorizes both psychiatric treatment and conventional criminal sanctions for defendants whose criminal offense was determined by their insanity. Verdict was introduced due to the increased number of crimes committed by defendants who had been previously released as not guilty by reason of insanity (NGRI)
