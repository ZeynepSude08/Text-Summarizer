----- ÖZET -----
The project aims to explore the questions behind people’s sexual orientation identification and the reasons behind it. Some people consider sexuality a subject of choice; others argue that the environment cannot dictate a person what sex attracts them. The following dependant and independent variables related to the topic were identified with the help of the GSS database.
