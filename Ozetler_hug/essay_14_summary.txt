“A Garden Still Life with a Kitten” Painting by Rosa Mayreder was painted in 1891. It depicts a small garden next to a house wall with a variety of plants in pots and a little kitten. The painting’s center of gravity on a horizontal axis is to the left, save for one. The horizontal shelf separates the most of the pots, which relate to each other as roughly 1.6 to 1.
