The pituitary gland is a major gland of the endocrine system. It excretes hormones in charge of other endocrine organs and different tissues within the body. This section aims to discuss anterior and posterior lobe hormones, explain what triggers the release of these hormones, and classify which hormones are considered tropic hormones.
