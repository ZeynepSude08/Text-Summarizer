Eat Lancet recommendations are developed to integrate healthy eating habits with practices that minimize the harm to the planet Earth resulting from some foods’ manufacturing or farming approaches. This paper will examine Lancet’s EAT recommendations, compare this diet to personal eating habits, and discuss whether modifications can be made.
