Allergic rhinitis is an intermittent or persistent inflammation of the mucous membrane of the upper respiratory tract. It is characterized by nasal congestion, discharge, itching, sneezing, and a combination of several symptoms. The hypersensitivity reaction is the first type since allergic rhinita is determined by it.
