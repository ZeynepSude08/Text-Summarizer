Critical thinking helps one gain control over their life, deal with life’s challenging situations, and make a rational decisions. The steps of critical thinking are; identifying the problem, detaching from emotional involvement, and determining solution criteria. They also include gathering and evaluating information, considering personal values and beliefs, and choosing and evaluating the solution.
