----- ÖZET -----
Artesunate is a first-line treatment followed by an entire course of malaria treatment. The drug is administered intravenously at 0, 12, 24 hours, and after that – daily. Recommended doses of Artesunate are 2.4 mg per kg of body weight for adults and children are heavier than 20 kilograms.
