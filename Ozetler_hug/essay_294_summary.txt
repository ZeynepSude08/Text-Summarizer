Equine influenza is a respiratory disease that is highly contagious to horses and other animals in the horse family. It is caused by numerous influenza subtypes, a virus comparable to but distinct from influenza viruses seen in other animals. The essay will discuss the effects, ways of transmission, and how to treat and prevent equine influenza.
