----- ÖZET -----
The Poem of The Righteous Sufferer is a Mesopotamian poem about the unjust suffering of a suffering man. The story is told in the first person, who honors his gods, but suffers from many illnesses. The Poem is a reflection of alienation from the divine, drawing attention to injustice and the problem of morality.
